About Us

About Alpana Academy

Welcome to Alpana Academy, where the timeless grace of Indian classical dance, with a primary focus on Kathak, blends seamlessly with the fluidity of contemporary expressions. We have embarked on a journey of rebranding, symbolizing a new chapter in our legacy.

Our Journey

Once known as Charulata Academy, we have a profound history rooted in the rich cultural tapestry of India and Bangladesh. Just as the Bollywood film industry has preserved and celebrated Indian culture, our academy has been a torchbearer for art and heritage, bridging the cultural heritage of both nations. It's no surprise that Devdas, a cinematic masterpiece, was penned by the renowned Bengali writer Sharat Chandra Chattopadhyay. At Alpana Academy, our mission is to provide an immersive and educational experience that beautifully encapsulates the essence of Indian and Bangladeshi art, culture, and heritage. Our accomplished and experienced teachers impart knowledge in various art forms, including dance, singing, drama, instrumental music, and workshops. Beyond the classroom, you'll have the opportunity to witness live theatrical concerts and events, creating an enriching and holistic learning experience.

Inclusivity and Vision

We believe that art and culture should be accessible to all. That's why we offer some of our classes, such as Bengali lessons for beginners to advanced and Yoga classes for women, completely free of charge. As a not-for-profit organization, we are dedicated to preserving, promoting, and sharing classical Indian and Bangladeshi art, culture, and heritage with as many people as possible. Our vision is clear – to educate, inspire, and engage our audience in appreciating the beauty of storytelling through various art forms, from ancient epics to contemporary tales. We aim to shine a light on the hidden treasures of our cultural heritage and make the world understand and cherish what we hold dear.

Alpana Academy, a hub of cultural richness, offers Kathak dance, diverse forms, expert instructors, and inclusivity—a place where tradition, innovation, and community unite in rhythmic harmony.
